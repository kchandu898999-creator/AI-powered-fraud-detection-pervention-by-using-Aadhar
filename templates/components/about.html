RakshaUID

RakshaUID is a document verification platform developed to combat identity fraud. We use a multi-layered artificial intelligence pipeline to verify Aadhaar cards with unmatched accuracy.

Deep Learning classification
OCR-based extraction
QR code validation
Checksum validation
Image forensic analysis
ML fraud scoring
  • Aadhaar Authentication
  • AI Fraud Detection
  • Instant Verification

Our Approach

Unlike single-step systems, we follow a stepwise, explainable approach. Documents are first analyzed visually using Deep Learning. Only after passing this check does the system proceed with deeper OCR extraction, QR validation, image forensics, and fraud scoring.

Our Aim

  • Fraud prevention
  • Automation of document verification
  • Reducing human error
  • Strengthening trust in digital identity
